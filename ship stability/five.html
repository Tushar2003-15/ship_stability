5. A rectangular tank measuring 25mx 12m x8m has an ullage pipe projecting 0.3m above the tank top. find the mass of SW in the tank when the ullage is 3.3m.
Solution :
Volume of the rectangular tank = ( L x B x H) = (25 x 12 x 8 )
Pipe above the tank top is 0.3m
Total ullage =3.3m
Ullage inside the tank = (3.3 – 0.3) = 3m
Depth of the SW = (8 – 3) = 5m
Volume of the SW = (L x B x H ) = ( 25 x12 x5) = 1500 m3
Mass of the SW = (volume x density) = (1500 x1.025) = 1537.5 t
