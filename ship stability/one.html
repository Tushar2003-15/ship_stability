1. A rectangular tank measures 16mx15mx6m. How many tonnes of oil of RD 0.78 can it hold?
SOLUTION:
Given : L =16m ,
B = 15m,
H = 6m,
RD=0.78
Volume of rectangle =(LxBxH)
=16x15x6=1440m3
Density=Mass /Volume
0.78=Mass/1440m3
Hence, Mass =1123.2 tonnes
